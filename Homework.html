What is Geography and why is it important?

Want to learn more about Geography? Visitthis website.

Geography has 3 branches one being physical geography talking about the actual earth and environment by itself. Another one is human geography which studies humans on where and how they live and settle and make industrys. And the last one is environmental geography which mixes both of human and physical geography. Geographers around the world get many job oppurtunites such as biologist, weather forecaster, city planner and many more!
